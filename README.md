## 📂 Projects

### 🏠 Airbnb Pricing Analysis
Statistical analysis of 100,000+ listings using Python. Applied Pearson correlation, ANOVA, Chi-Square, and Kruskal-Wallis tests to identify key pricing drivers such as service fee and cancellation policy.
`Python` `pandas` `SciPy` `matplotlib` `seaborn`

---

### 🌫️ Air Quality PCA
Dimensionality reduction on the UCI Air Quality dataset (6,900+ hourly observations). Reduced 12 sensor variables to 4 principal components explaining 93% of total variance.
`Python` `NumPy` `scikit-learn` `matplotlib` `seaborn`

---

### 🔐 RC4 Cipher Cryptanalysis
Research into RC4 vulnerabilities in WEP and SSL/TLS protocols, including analysis of the Fluhrer-Mantin-Shamir (FMS) attack and recommended countermeasures.
`Cryptography` `Security Analysis`

---

### 📊 Sales Data Dashboard *(Power BI)*

`Power BI` `DAX` `Data Modelling`
